EYE CHECK CAMP


The objective of this event is to spread awareness on eye disorders, their early detection and to detect refractive errors and eye ailments among people.

HEALTH CHECK-UP CAMPS


The general health check up of the patients will be done in the camp. Medicines will also be provided to the patients. A friendly interaction will be done with the paitent and with the community people making them aware of health care.
